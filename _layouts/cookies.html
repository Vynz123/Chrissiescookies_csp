Our Story

It all started with one big dream, five teens, and the perfect combination of flour, sugar, and chocolate chips. Chrissie's Cookies was co-founded by Aashray, Arushi, Chrissie, Harkirat, and Shubhay. They teamed up and dove head-first into the world of baking. After thousands in wasted dough, recipes that did not live up to their expetations, and cookies that are just plain embarrassing to them today, the five teens decided to take their 'perfect cookie quest' to the people. They gathered feedback and tested recipes -- a practice that is part of the process -- until they created the world's best chocolate chip cookie
